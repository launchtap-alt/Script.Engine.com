Script Breakdown

Every time you fill the brief above, three complete scripts come out. Here is what each one does and what to ask yourself before you hit generate.

Script 01 · Concept
What it is and why it exists
Your teaching piece. Pure signal. Introduce the concept in plain language and plant it in your audience's mind. The goal is clarity. Make them feel like they finally understand something they were confused about before they found you.
Can I explain this in one sentence to someone with zero experience?
What is the single most important thing about this topic?
What changes for someone who learns this versus someone who never does?
Script 02 · Tip
The thing most people get wrong
Your authority piece. One sharp, specific, actionable insight that sounds simple but changes how people think and execute. Not a list. One thing said with total precision. The best tips feel like insider knowledge they can use today.
What mistake do beginners make with this concept?
What would I do differently now that I understand this?
What is the one rule I would give someone starting from zero?
Script 03 · Story
Make it real and make it felt
Your connection piece. Take the concept out of theory and drop it into a scene. A local business. A familiar situation. A before and after. The audience does not just understand it. They feel it. Stories are what people share. Data is what people forget.
What everyday scenario does this remind me of?
What does before learning this look like versus after?
Who in my audience most needs to hear this story?
